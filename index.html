About Me

Introduction: Jhuma Sarkar

Jhuma Sarkar is a Post Graduate in Human Resources and an SHRM- Senior Certified Professional, which is an Internationally acclaimed HR certification having acceptance in over 160 countries globally. She has also done her Executive Development Program from XLRI in HR Analytics.
She brings on table over 17 years of core HR experience from diverse industries with her core strength of working with Start- Ups from Grass Root level.
She has empaneled latest HR systems to ensure that the processes are benchmarked as per the latest Industry trends. She has credit of handling array of HR assignments like Design and Re-design of entire HR policies and systems, HR Audit, Compensation Surveys, setting up end to end HR system for new turnkey Projects, Compensation and Payroll revamp with key focus on taxation management which a rare combination in HR.
She has accolades such as Women Achiever Award from “Genius HR Excellence Awards” for the year 2016 & 2018; “World HRD Congress” forum, 2017. She also offers session from University of Kerala as a Guest Speaker to final year students.
